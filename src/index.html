VitraHaus is a virtual, interactive and generative Museum of generative works, generated — Based on the architecture of Herzog & de Meuron’s VitraHaus, this building exhibits the work of several visual artists.

The project was made in 2020 by four interactive design and development students at the Gobelins Shcool (Paris, FR) in a workshop of two weeks.

Design : Bastien Maillotte & Lucas Perret.
Development : Leith Ben Abdessalem & Lissandre Pasdeloup.
